Find Your Niche

The 5 Investor Paths

Most investors waste years because they chose the wrong path. We identify yours based on your schedule, income, and risk tolerance — then build your plan around it.

Fix & Flip

Buy undervalued properties, renovate, and sell for profit. Fast returns, high activity — best for hands-on investors with strong project management skills.

Wholesale

Find deeply discounted properties and assign contracts to buyers — no renovation, no ownership. The fastest path to your first deal with little to no capital required.

Rental

Buy and hold residential or multi-family properties for long-term cash flow and appreciation. The classic wealth-building path — steady passive income month after month.

Land

Buy raw or undeveloped land at deep discounts and sell to builders or developers. Low competition, low overhead, and surprisingly high margins for patient investors.

Commercial

Office, retail, industrial, or mixed-use properties. Higher barriers to entry but significantly larger returns and longer-term leases that lock in income for years.
